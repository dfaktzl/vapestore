Annual Budget Breakdown: Tobacco Taxes vs Disposable Costs

Published: 2026-07-08
Author: AeroVape Academy
Reading Time: 1 min read

Due to ongoing federal excise tax increases on tobacco, Australia has some of the highest cigarette prices globally. Transitioning to disposables or pod systems provides substantial financial savings. Let's analyze the annual budget difference between smoking and vaping.

Cost Breakdown of Traditional Tobacco

Assuming a consumption rate of one 20-pack of standard cigarettes per day:

  • Average Pack Cost: $45.00 AUD
  • Weekly Cost: $315.00 AUD
  • Monthly Cost: $1,350.00 AUD
  • Annual Expenditure: $16,425.00 AUD

Cost Breakdown of Disposables (AeroVape Carton Pricing)

Assuming one high-capacity disposable vape (such as an IGET Bar 3500) lasts approximately 5–7 days for an average user:

  • Single Unit Cost (Wholesale Carton Rate): $26.00 AUD
  • Weekly Cost: $26.00 to $36.00 AUD
  • Monthly Cost: $104.00 to $150.00 AUD
  • Annual Expenditure: $1,248.00 to $1,800.00 AUD
