Extending Coil Life: The Complete Guide to Maintenance

Published: 2026-07-01
Author: AeroVape Academy
Reading Time: 1 min read

Maximizing the lifespan of your heating elements is essential for maintaining clean flavor profiles and preventing dry draws. Over regular usage, caramelized e-liquid components, organic sweeteners, and flavor concentrates form a thick residue (often referred to as 'coil gunk') on the metal wire and cotton wick. This residue restricts airflow, decreases vapor production, and ruins the taste of your draw.

Benefits of Coil Maintenance

  • Flavor Clarity: Freshly rinsed wicks deliver clean flavor without background notes of burnt sweetener.
  • Increased Lifespan: Removing debris prevents hot spots from developing and burning out the organic cotton.
  • Cost Efficiency: Regular maintenance reduces your annual expenditure on replacement coils.

Step-by-Step Maintenance Protocol

  1. Extraction: Allow your tank to cool, disassemble the threads, and unscrew the coil head.
  2. Warm Water Rinse: Run warm tap water through the center of the coil to flush out loose syrup and residue.
  3. Deep Cleaning Soak: Submerge the coil in a bowl of warm water or high-proof ethanol (such as unflavored vodka) for 1–2 hours. The alcohol acts as a solvent to dissolve caramelized sugars.
  4. Final Rinse: If alcohol was used, thoroughly rinse the coil with clean warm water.
  5. Extended Air Dry: Place the coil on a clean microfiber towel. Allow it to dry naturally for at least 24 hours. Attempting to fire a wet coil will cause hot water spitting and potential short circuits.
  6. Priming: Saturate the exposed cotton wicks with 3–5 drops of e-liquid, reassemble the tank, fill it, and let it stand for 10 minutes before firing.
