Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay for my order?

We accept PayID and Direct Bank Transfer only. After placing your order, you'll receive full payment instructions including our BSB, account number, and PayID email. Orders are dispatched once payment is confirmed — usually within minutes for PayID.

How fast is delivery?

We ship via Express Post Australia-wide. Most orders arrive within 1–3 business days after payment is confirmed. Orders over $150 receive free express shipping (orders under $150 incur a flat $15 shipping fee).

Are there any minimum order quantities?

No minimum for single vapes or cigarette cartons — you can order just one. Wholesale box products (HQD Cuvie Bar, Waka SoPro) are sold as full boxes of 10 units only. Mix and match any products in one order to hit the $150 free shipping threshold.
